I am a designer, pattern-maker, sewist, and an all-purpose crafter and maker.

I use all my own patterns, based on a sloper, which is a basic pattern I created from my specific measurements. Using the sloper I can create any pattern, with my own measurements already built in.

Sewing is my primary medium, but I also dabble in quilting, macrame, weaving, and web-design.

Find me on Facebook and Instagram, or contact me at annawflowers@gmail.com.
