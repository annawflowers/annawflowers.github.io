Chambray Romper: Summer 2019

I started working on this romper in August of 2018 - it always seems to be the ends of seasons when the ideas start flowing. I got a bit bored of working on it as it got colder, and ended up not picking it up again in earnest until June of 2019. I think it came out really sweet in the end!

This romper is made from a light cotton chambray, perfect for midsummer. The shorts have slash pockets and close with a zip, and the top has an open tie back. I love the look of full coverage in the front with an open back.
